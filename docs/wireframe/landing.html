Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VocabBuilder.

How does spaced repetition work?

Spaced repetition is a learning technique that reviews material at increasing intervals. When you rate a card as "Easy," you won't see it again for days. If you rate it "Again," you'll review it soon. This optimizes your study time and maximizes long-term retention.

Can I create my own vocabulary lists?

Absolutely! You can enter any topic, paste text, or type specific words. Our AI will generate contextual vocabulary cards automatically. You can also manually edit cards, add notes, and organize them into custom collections.

How much time do I need to dedicate daily?

Just 5-10 minutes per day is enough to make significant progress. Our system prioritizes due cards efficiently, so you can complete your daily reviews in a short focused session. Consistency matters more than duration.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your learning data is encrypted and never shared with third parties. You own your vocabulary collections and can export them at any time. We're committed to protecting your privacy.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period. We also offer a 14-day money-back guarantee.
